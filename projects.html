Spring 2019

For my undergraduate capstone, my team of 5 developed an autonomous robot that can automatically adhere to and navigate over a window pane, cross over a barrier, and clean a 5' x 6' total area in under 3 minutes. The device uses ducted propellers to adhere to the window, wheels to move, and a sponge-based mechanism for cleaning. Proximity sensors and an IMU help the robot to navigate. I was in charge of assembling the electronic components and circuitry of the robot. I also helped to develop the control, locomotion, adhesion, sensing, and cleaning subsystems.

More details about this project can be found on our capstone website.
